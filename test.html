Our Story

A Cozy Corner in IIT Bombay

Founded in 2025, 9Tea7 Shack has become the go-to spot for students, faculty, and locals alike. Our mission is simple: serve quality tea and snacks with warmth and comfort.

The 9Tea7 Shack, a vibrant new café within the Project Evergreen Complex, made possible with support of the Class of 1997.

The playful name '9Tea7' is a tribute to their light-hearted spirit and unforgettable campus memories.
