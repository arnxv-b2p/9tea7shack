Our Story

A Cozy Corner in IIT Bombay

Founded in 2025, 9Tea7 Shack has become the go-to spot for students, faculty, and locals alike. Our mission is simple: serve quality tea and snacks with a side of warmth and comfort.

The 9Tea7 Shack, a vibrant new café within the Project Evergreen Complex, made possible with the generous support of the Class of 1997.

The playful name '9Tea7' is a tribute to the Class of 1997's light-hearted spirit, ensuring future generations know this space was created by alumni who valued those moments of respite, laughter, and endless cacking that made campus life unforgettable.
